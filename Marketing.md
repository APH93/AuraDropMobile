auradrop water tracker

A simple daily water tracker for building better hydration habits.

Track every glass, set a daily goal, view your recent history, build streaks, and keep your progress visible with Home Screen widgets.

Features

- Quick glass logging
- Daily hydration goal
- Current and best streak tracking
- Calendar history
- Hourly, 2-hour, and 3-hour reminders
- Custom reminder start and end times
- Home Screen widgets
- Shareable streak cards
- Optional tip jar to support development

Designed for everyday use

auradrop water tracker keeps hydration tracking simple. No account required, no complicated setup, and no unnecessary clutter.

Privacy first

Your hydration data stays on your device. The app does not sell or share your personal data.
