Privacy Policy for auradrop water tracker

auradrop water tracker is designed as a simple hydration tracking app. Your privacy is important to us.

**Information We Collect:**

auradrop water tracker does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

The app does not require an account. Hydration logs, daily goals, reminder settings, streaks, and widget data are stored locally on your device. The app may use an App Group container so the main app and Home Screen widget can stay in sync on the same device.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, auradrop water tracker uses local notifications to remind you to drink water. Notification settings remain on your device and can be changed or disabled at any time in the app or iOS Settings.

**In-App Purchases:**

The app includes optional tips to support development. Payments are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment details.

**Data Sharing**

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

**Children’s Privacy**

auradrop water tracker does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

**Changes to This Policy**

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
