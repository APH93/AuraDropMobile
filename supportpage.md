**Common Questions**

Widgets are not updating

Open the app once after installing or updating. Then remove and re-add the widget from the Home Screen. Make sure the app and widget are installed from the same version.

Reminders are not working

Check that notifications are enabled for auradrop water tracker in iOS Settings. Also confirm your reminder start time, end time, and interval inside the app.

Tips are not loading

Make sure you are connected to the internet and signed in to the App Store. In-app purchases are handled by Apple.

Data and privacy

auradrop water tracker stores hydration logs locally on your device. The app does not require an account and does not sell your data.
